Ali Scott Mirbagheri

Associate

Ali Scott Mirbagheri is an associate at Mirbagheri & Smith. He received his Bachelor’s degree in Mathematics from the University of California, Riverside in 1986 and his J.D. from the University of California, Davis, in 1990. He was formerly an Asylum Officer with the Immigration and Nationality Service (today’s USCIS) and a Project Manager for a private firm providing litigation support to the Department of Justice. Mr. Mirbagheri is admitted to the state bar of California. He has spent many years overseas and is fluent in English, French, Farsi, and Arabic.
