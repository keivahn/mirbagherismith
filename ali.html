Ali Nasserghodsi

Associate

Ali Nasserghodsi joined the Mirbagheri & Smith team in December of 2012 after earning his J.D. degree from the University of Baltimore School of Law and is now a member of the Maryland State Bar. Prior to pursuing his J.D., he received his bachelor’s degree in psychology from the University of Maryland, College Park, in 2007. He was a member of the Immigrant Rights Clinic at the Baltimore School of Law where he protected the rights of indigent and vulnerable populations. Mr. Nasserghodsi has extensive experience in all family-based and consular processing cases, including assisting with adopted children becoming permanent residents and citizens. He has expertise in humanitarian reinstatement requests, affidavits of support, H2A agricultural workers, and the obtainment of special licenses from the Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC). Mr. Nasserghodsi is bilingual in English and Farsi.
